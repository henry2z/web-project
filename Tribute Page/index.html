Albert Einstein

"I am at the mercy of fate and have no control over it"
  • 1879 -- Albert Einstein is born, March 14, in Germany
  • 1881 -- Albert Einstein's sister Maja Einstein is born
  • 1899 -- Albert Einstein decides to become a Swiss citizen
  • 1900 -- Albert Einstein graduates from college
  • 1903 -- Albert Einstein marries Mileva Maric
  • 1914 -- Albert leaves Mileva Maric and his sons and move to Berlin, Germany
  • 1919 -- Albert Einstein marries Elsa Einstein
  • 1936 -- Death of Elsa Einstein
  • 1940 -- Albert Einstein becomes a U.S. citizen
  • 1948 -- Death of Mileva Maric
  • 1951 -- Death of Maja Einstein
  • 1955 -- Albert Einstein dies on April 18
